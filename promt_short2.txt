Online Food Ordering System
Version 1.0
Date: November 2, 2025

1. Purpose
This document specifies the requirements for an Online Food Ordering System that allows customers to place and manage food orders through a website and mobile app.

2. Scope
The system enables customers to browse restaurant menus, place orders, and make payments. Restaurants can receive and process orders through their management dashboard.

3. Functional Requirements

FR1: Users can register, log in, and view past orders.
FR2: Users can only place orders after payment is confirmed.
FR3: The system shall allow users to pay after delivery to ensure satisfaction.
FR4: Restaurants can update menu items and set availability status.
FR5: The admin can remove any restaurant from the system.

4. Non-Functional Requirements

NFR1: The system should process all orders in under 3 seconds.
NFR2: The system must be accessible on both web and mobile platforms.

User Story 1:As a customer, I want to pay online before ordering so that my order is processed immediately.
User Story 2:As a customer, I want to pay after I receive my food so I can make sure it’s correct and fresh.
User Story 3:As a restaurant owner, I want to automatically reject unpaid orders to avoid fake or spam orders.
User Story 4:As a restaurant owner, I want the system to auto-accept all orders so customers don’t have to wait for manual confirmation.
User Story 5:As an admin, I want to allow restaurants to temporarily close during off-hours but also keep accepting pre-orders anytime.
